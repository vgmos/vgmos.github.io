I am Vasu Gupta, an analog IC design engineer with a long-standing interest in circuits, computation, memory, and the ways technical work shapes a person.

My older academic work moved through BITS Pilani, Technion, and Georgia Tech. That chapter mostly closed around 2021, but the projects still deserve a better record than scattered files and fading memory. I am restoring them here as a project archive: part technical documentation, part retrospective.

The newer writing will be broader. Some notes will be technical. Some will be about craft, discipline, attention, faith, spirituality, relationships, and the inner weather of becoming an adult with a serious professional life. The aim is not a personal brand. It is a useful, honest record.

You can find me on [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/vasugupta711) and [GitHub](https://github.com/vgmos).
