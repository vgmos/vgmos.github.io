I’m a senior analog IC designer at Analog Devices. My work focuses on low-noise power management: DC-DC converters for RF, OLED, and automotive systems. The projects below are longer write-ups from my past research in power conversion, data converters, and device modeling; the tools are small circuit-design references and calculators. The notebook is for whatever doesn’t fit a project page. Some of it is technical, some of it isn’t.
